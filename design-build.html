Permits and Approval required for renovation in NYC

Work Permit by Department of Buildings

A work permit from the NYC Department of Building entails that the renovation projects meet the building codes.

Electrical and Plumbing Permits

ATOZ Renovations’ legal team also handles obtaining and filing electrical and plumbing permits from NYC DOB

Landmark Application

Most Brownstone houses in NYC are so old that they have acquired the status of Landmark by LPC. Construction and renovation of such sites are regulated by the Dept. of LPC. In this case, the renovator has to get approval for all exterior materials and design aesthetics from the LPC.
