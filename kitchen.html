01
Phase 01
Pricing & Planning

Your renovation journey begins with a meeting with our renovation experts. Your remodeling Expert will discuss your ideas, wants, and aspirations for your home, as well as any potential challenges that may occur during the remodeling process, during the planning session. They will also collaborate with you to develop a realistic budget that accounts for all prospective costs, such as materials, labor, and permit fees. Once the plan is finalized, you may begin the renovation process with the knowledge that you have made the first step toward constructing the hyour dreams.

02
STEP 02
Legal Permits

Our legal team has extensive experience navigating the complexities of NYC’s ever-changing renovation laws. We have established a streamlined network to get all legal approval and permits from authorities, leaving you with nothing but to sign off and receive your approval. We ensure that the Legal approval seeking process runs smoothly and efficiently while keeping it cost-effective

03
STEP 03
Design

In the designing stage, one of our expert designers meets with you to discuss the development and scope of this renovation project. Our designer designs a schematic chart with you to get your dream on paper before it dives into construction. During this phase, our designer will provide you with a detailed blueprint of the renovation plan, as well as ideas for how to incorporate your specific style into the design. The designer will also provide you with guidance on the materials, colors, and other elements that will be used in the renovation project. This is the last step before your project moves into Construction.

04
STEP 04
Construction

At the Construction stage, your old structure is demolished. And we procured and install new material in your system. Also, we upgrade Electrical and plumbing fixtures to support the structure of your new home. Next new windows and doors are installed. A full structure remodeling is done to renovate every corner of your home according to your ideas and aesthetics. Lastly, carpentry, wall painting, and new furniture installation are done to bring your dream home into a living breathing place.

05
STEP 05
Final Reveal

Once all renovations are according to your requirements, and everything in your new home is in its new place and functional; a final reveal walk-in is scheduled with you. During this walk-in, the team will walk you through the details of the design and construction of your new home. This includes the material used, the structural changes, the finesse of the craftsmanship, and all other meticulous detailings; that were made to transform your dream into a reality
