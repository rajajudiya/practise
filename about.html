About Us

We embrace the profound reality that a home is not just an ordinary place; it embodies the essence of its residents’ personalities. It is a place where dreams are nurtured, ambitions are realized and greatness is forged. It is also the most prized possession most of us hold. Therefore it is equally important for us. Designing homes is not just our business, it is our passion. We put our blood and sweat to bring your vision to life.

OUR STORY

We are a Design-Build firm located in the heart of NYC and Brooklyn. At ATOZ Renovations we offer the finest level of service to our customers. From start to finish, we partner with our clients to Design and Build the home of their dreams. Our remodeling crew is experienced and skilled in their area of expertise, guaranteeing that all work is completed efficiently and on time. We also offer legal support in obtaining the relevant permits and documents. In addition, our showroom contains a wide range of products, from fixtures and gadgets to appliances and flooring. Finally, our interior designers are available to discuss your ideas and assist you in designing the home of your dreams. This is how we design the perfect homes for our customers. And we are proud of what we do, and how we do it.
